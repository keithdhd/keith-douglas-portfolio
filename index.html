Introduction

I'm a programmer, musician and writer and I'm updating my coding skills in order to find a good job in an awesome company.

Working with and for smart, passionate people is probably my main goal at the moment. I've also dabbled in affiliate marketing and learnt a lot about how (and how not) to build websites that people respond to and Google loves (or hates).
